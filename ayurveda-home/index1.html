Oils (External)

Therapeutic oils, crafted by blending herbs in sesame oil, coconut oil, or milk, are applied through skin or body openings, promoting relaxation, pain relief, and holistic healing for enhanced wellness.

Oils (Internal)

Ayurvedic oils taken orally enhance digestion, elimination, respiratory, sinus, nasal and throat health. Concentrated herbal formulations rejuvenate the entire body, promoting holistic wellness and delivering significant therapeutic benefits.

Liquids

Herbal fermented liquids are prepared by boiling herbal extracts or decoctions, followed by fermentation. Naturally preserved, these formulations deliver effective therapeutic benefits when consumed orally for digestion and overall wellness.

Jams

Traditional Ayurvedic herbal jams, made by boiling herbal decoctions with sugar, jaggery, or honey, are sweet, nutrient-rich syrups that provide rejuvenation, vitality, and nourishment for the body and mind.

Powders

Ayurvedic powders, crafted from herbs and minerals, offer nourishing and cleansing benefits. They can be consumed orally as supplements or applied externally to specific body parts for targeted health benefits.

Ghees

Herbal ghee, enriched with potent herbs, supports digestion, gastrointestinal health and elimination. Consumed orally or applied to the eyes, it enhances overall wellness and balances the body's natural processes.

Pills & Tablets

Herbal pills, tablets and capsules contain oils, powders or liquids. They provide a convenient way to integrate therapeutic Ayurvedic remedies into daily routines for internal use and holistic health.

Miscellaneous

A wide variety of Ayurvedic products, including herbal drinks, kitcheri meals, pain relief balms, purified ghee and Ayurveda treatment accessories, promote balanced lifestyles and support holistic health and vitality.
