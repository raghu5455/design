OVER 20 YEARS OF DEDICATED SERVICE

D&W Consulting has established itself as a premier GIS consulting firm with an esteemed reputation in the industry. Our experienced principals have successfully delivered exceptional GIS solutions to more than 150 public agencies, ranging from small-scale initiatives to large, world-class endeavors.

At D&W Consulting, we prioritize a client-centric approach, beginning with a thorough understanding of our clients' needs. We specialize in crafting tailored plans that precisely address these requirements. Once a plan is finalized, we execute it with meticulous project management, ensuring superior product quality and maintaining transparent communication throughout the process.

This methodology guarantees that every project we undertake achieves its objectives efficiently and to the highest satisfaction of our clients. We offer comprehensive system and data integration services that cover the entire project life-cycle. From initial needs assessment to full-scale implementation and ongoing operations, our expertise ensures the seamless integration of GIS solutions with enterprise technologies, delivering robust and effective systems and applications.

Andrea Rosas

With 8 years of experience as a GIS Manager, Andrea Rosas has developed a robust skill set in understanding client needs and translating them into effective GIS applications. Her expertise includes working with tools like Survey123, Dashboards, Experience Builder, and ArcGIS Online to create customized solutions. Additionally, her ability to manage complex demographic analyses and integrate various systems, such as Placer.ai with GIS, has been key in enhancing operational efficiencies and data-driven decision-making.

Andrea’s technical proficiency extends to advanced tools and platforms such as SQL Server, Python, and Alteryx, which she leverages for data management and automation. She also has experience with Adobe Suite (Illustrator & Photoshop) for high-quality visual outputs, and has worked with 3D GIS modeling tools like Matterport and FARO to deliver detailed spatial analyses. Her comprehensive understanding of GIS and related technologies has made her a valuable asset in facilities management and other GIS-driven projects.

Kyle Profile

With nine years of experience in GIS, Kyle Monte has developed a robust skill set through diverse and challenging roles. His career began with four years in the Army Corps of Engineers, where he served as a GIS Engineer and spent a year in Iraq. During this time, Kyle conducted critical analyses of strategic security locations for both the Iraqi and United States military, created professional resources for navigation, and supported training events in a combat environment. This experience refined his ability to give presentations to command as the GIS specialist in charge. This allowed SGT. Monte to exceed client expectations, perform detailed fieldwork, and manage data efficiently under high-stress conditions.

In addition to his military experience, Kyle has gained proficiency in a range of GIS tools and technologies, including ArcGIS Pro, Survey123, ESRI Business Analyst, and Matterport Scanning. His skill set also includes field surveying, route planning, and situation analysis, highlighting his capacity to integrate complex spatial data into actionable insights. This blend of practical field experience and technical expertise equips Kyle to tackle complex GIS projects and deliver precise, impactful solutions within tight and critical schedules.

Jerry Wagner

Jerry A. Wagner brings over 40 years of expertise in developing and implementing Geographic Information Systems (GIS), specializing in government, utility, retail and property management GIS. His extensive experience includes designing and managing the implementation of complex GIS solutions, notably overseeing the building of the facilities GIS for the Irvine Company’s Retail Division which manages over 40 shopping centers. Jerry excels in enterprise GIS implementation, organizational consulting, and comprehensive requirements analysis. With a background in Photogrammetry, he has a unique way of understanding accuracy requirements to make projects successful. Jerry's strengths lie in effectively communicating with technical teams and management, integrating geographic information into decision support systems, and delivering high-quality GIS products crucial for informed decision-making.
