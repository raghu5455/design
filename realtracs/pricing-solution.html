When you sign up for Realtracs, you are signing up for committed and personalized customer service. Every office we work with is assigned to a member of our Customer Engagement Team. The goal is exactly what it sounds like — to ensure our customers are happy and well taken care of. Our Customer Engagement Team makes office visits, provides one-on-one assistance, and helps our users navigate questions and challenges with our system. Realtracs users will never have to wonder where to turn for support.

RealtracsONE is a unified search tool that consolidates MLS lis9ng search and reporting into a single location for the MLS to which an end-user belongs (i.e., has an active membership or subscription). It allows a real estate professional to see all their data in ONE place.

Brokers and agents who have a membership or subscription with Realtracs and another eligible MLS.

To register for RealtracsONE, a customer visits their Data Access settings page in Realtracs to request access. By submitting a request, the customer agrees that an ac9ve, paid membership with the requested MLS is in place and that their first name, last name, and agent license number are up to date in both Realtracs and the requested MLS.  Once requested, Realtracs then verifies there is an active membership with the MLS(s). If active and valid, Realtracs requests a par9cipant data access (PDAP) and broker back office (BBO) license on the broker and/or agent’s behalf (when and where applicable).
