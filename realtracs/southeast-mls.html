Access listing data within a new network of the largest MLSs in

IN PARTNERSHIP WITH

More Exposure Than Ever Before

Bringing together data from the largest MLSs across four states in
collaboration with founding members.

The Southeast MLS Alliance grants access to a greater property marketplace across 6 states in collaboration with CHS Regional MLS, Canopy MLS, and Georgia MLS. All Realtracs' subscriptions include access to the Southeast MLS Alliance within their plans, giving subscribers a broader area and greater exposure to listings.

FAQ

The Southeast MLS Alliance is a collaboration between Canopy MLS (Charlotte) Charleston Regional MLS, and Georgia MLS.

Yes. All Realtracs users have access to the Southeast MLS Alliance and can search properties within the data share in one platform.

The alliance was established to provide real estate agents across MLS services with more data and potential for referrals. It allows agents to work together across state lines and better serve their clients.
